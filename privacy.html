Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

1. Overview

Overdraft Content Manager ("the App") is a personal content management tool. It accesses your TikTok account solely to upload video drafts on your behalf.

2. Data Collection

This application collects only the minimum data required for TikTok API authentication (OAuth tokens). No personal data is collected, sold, or shared with third parties.

3. Data Usage

Authentication tokens are stored locally on your device and used exclusively to upload content drafts to your TikTok account. No analytics, tracking, or telemetry data is collected.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally. No data is transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints during the upload process.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with TikTok's Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.
